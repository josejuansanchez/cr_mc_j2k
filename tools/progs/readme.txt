This directory includes the following applications:

FSVC: Fully Scalable Video Coding and decoding.
dwt: The Discrete Wavelet Transform and the Redundant DWT.
mux: Multiplexation and demultiplexation of data.
scalar: Simple mathematical operations over scalar data.
snr: Compute the Signal to Noise Ratio.
type: Native data type conversions.
video_show: A simple application to visualize RAW video sequences.
vix2raw: Converts VIX video sequences to RAW video sequences.
rgb2yuv: RGB to YUV and TYV to RGB video data conversion.
